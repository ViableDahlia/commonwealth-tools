# MO2 Mod List Reader

A lightweight browser-based tool for reading and exploring your **Mod Organizer 2** mod list. No install, no account, no data sent anywhere.

Built for Fallout 4 modders who want a fast, readable view of their `modlist.txt` outside of MO2 itself.

---

## What it does

- **Drag and drop** your `modlist.txt` straight into the browser
- See every mod in your list with its **enabled/disabled status** and **priority**
- **Search** by mod name with live highlighting
- **Filter** to show all mods, enabled only, or disabled only
- **Sort** by priority (ascending or descending), alphabetically, or by status
- Displays **stats** at a glance: total mods, enabled, disabled, and separators

Everything runs locally in your browser. Your mod list never leaves your machine.

---

## How to use it

**Step 1**: Open the tool in your browser

**Step 2**: Navigate to your MO2 profiles folder:
```
...\Mod Organizer 2\profiles\[YourProfileName]\modlist.txt
```

**Step 3**: Drag `modlist.txt` onto the page, or click **Browse File**

**Step 4**: Search, filter and sort your mod list

---

## Compatibility

- Works with any Mod Organizer 2 profile
- Tested with Fallout 4 (including Anniversary Edition)
- Should work with any game MO2 supports - the `modlist.txt` format is the same
